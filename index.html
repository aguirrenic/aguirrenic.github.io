Real-time industrial monitoring platform built using SQL, Amazon Redshift, and Grafana, designed to calculate and visualize Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE) in manufacturing environments.

What is OEE?

OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) is the standard metric used to measure manufacturing productivity. It identifies the percentage of manufacturing time that is truly productive. A 100% OEE score represents perfect Quality, Performance, and Availability.

Why is measuring OEE important?

  • Efficiency Identification: Detects productivity losses across production lines.
  • Benchmarking & Continuous Improvement: Enables long-term performance tracking.
  • Waste Reduction: Identifies downtime, speed losses, and quality defects.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Supports maintenance planning and capital investments.
